Chapter 3: The Speaking Trees

As you venture deeper into the Enchanted Forest, you come across a grove of ancient trees. Unlike ordinary trees, these seem to be whispering among themselves. Their leaves rustle even though there's no wind.

"Welcome, traveler," a deep voice echoes. It's coming from the largest tree in the center of the grove. "We are the Keepers of Forest Knowledge. What would you like to learn today?"

What will you do?
Ask about forest ecosystems

Learn about how different plants and animals interact in the forest environment.

Inquire about tree communication

Discover how trees communicate with each other through root systems and fungi networks.
